About Me

Hello! I am Mrinmoy Duarah, an award-winning actor known for my work in Assamese drama and cinema.

Biography

I was born on March 11, 2009. My passion for acting started at a young age, inspired by my father, who was also an actor. Currently, I am an active performer in UKA Drama Works, participating in prestigious drama competitions across Assam.
